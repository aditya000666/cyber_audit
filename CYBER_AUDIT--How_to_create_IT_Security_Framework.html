How to create IT Security Framework


• IT security falls within established IT policy framework.
• Policy framework provides structured approach for outlining requirements that must be met
• Framework starts on top with very clear and concise objective or requirements
• At topmost level is the policy which regulates conduct through general statement of belief, goal and objectives
• Standard support policies providing mandate activities or rule
• Guideline further support standard as well as policy providing general statement of guidance but these are not mandatory
