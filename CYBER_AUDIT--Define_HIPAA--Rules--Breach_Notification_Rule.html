Breach Notification Rule


• Breach Notification rule requires covered entities to notify affected individuals in breach of unsecured PHI
• In case of any breach HIPPA rules report to supervision or personal responsible for HIPPA policy
• Breach must be notified to affected individuals
• Business associate must notify covered entity when breach occurred
• Covered agency must notify affected individual by mail
• Such information conveyed within period of 60 days
• Information must include the extent a fair description of such breach, steps to be taken care by affected and what is done by agency to take care of breach
• Agency should post breach on website for 90 days
• Any such breach may be conveyed on phone to affected individual
• Both Civil and Criminal penalties may be result of HIPPA breach
