Personal Rights regarding PHI


• Patients can see copy of medical records that covered entity keeps
• Request for inaccurate PHI to be accurate
• Written statement can be submitted by patient to correct or add relevant information
• Patient PHI not disclose for payment or health care operation if payed for full service cost to PHI
• Patient can ask for copy of medical records
• May not be allowed to see certain information but may ask for correction of inaccurate records
