Teleworking Policy


• Policy and supporting security measures should be implemented to protect information accessed, processed or stored at teleworking sites
• Organisation allowing teleworking activities should issue policy that defines conditions and restriction for teleworking
• Teleworking policy should consider
• Existing physical security of teleworking site, taking into account physical security
• Proposed physical teleworking environment
• Communication security requirements taking into the need for remote access
• Provision of virtual desktop access that prevents processing and storage of information on privately owned equipment
• Threat of unauthorised access
• Use of home network and requirement or restriction on configuration of wireless network service
• Policies and procedures to prevent dispute concerning rights to intellectual property
• Access to privately owned equipment
• Software licensing agreements that are such organisation may become liable for licensing for client software
• Malware protection and firewall requirements
