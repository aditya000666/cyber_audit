How organisations maintain Compliance


• Organisations are dynamic. As they adapt and grow, things change and compliance must be assessed
• Threats evolve. Threats to organisation like organisation themselves constantly change and adapt
• Laws, regulations and industry standards continue to evolve and news one are introduced
• Many regulations require annual audits, ongoing report and regular assessments

• Achieving compliance with external standards and regulations is to be first consideration in assembling a policy infrastructure
• Being in compliance makes sure the organisation meets expectations of policy by enforcing infrastructure put into place
• Organisational policies provide general statements that address the operational goals of organisation
• Complying with organisation internal policy require standards
• Internal standards describe mandatory process or objectives that align with goal of policies
• Establishing policies and standards is critical for ensuring the success of organisation as well as compliance with regulations with which organisation must comply
• When policies and framework are in place organisation can start implementing specific controls
• These controls can further address to risks of the organisation
• Always consider reasonable and appropriate controls for the organisation
• Having sound policies in place and framework for application of controls, perform gap analysis to identify anything missing
