CAAT for Operational Review


• Technique to cover operational review as well as collect information about the effectiveness of general controls over IT operations
• Basic tools such as Microsoft Access used to import data of operational data and perform analysis
• Then moving the analysed data in Microsoft Excel and visually portray information for management or even forecast trends with regard to workload, growth and other IT operational areas
• Specific activities in operational review include
◇ Review operating policies and documentation
◇ Confirm procedures with management and operating personnel
◇ Observer operating functions and activities
◇ Examine financial and operating plans and reports
◇ Test accuracy of operating function
◇ Test operation controls
