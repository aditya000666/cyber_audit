Define NIST 800-53


• Provides comprehensive catalog of security controls
• NIST 800-53A provides framework for assessing the adequacy of in-place controls
• Address a wide of controls within the organisation
• Controls consider multiple aspects including management, technical, and operational
• Catalog of controls
◇ Access Control
◇ Awareness and Training
◇ Audit and Accountability
◇ Configuration Management
◇ Contingency Planning
◇ Identification and Authentication
◇ Incident Response
◇ Maintenance
◇ Media Protection
◇ Physical and Environmental Protection
◇ Planning
◇ Personnel Security
◇ Risk Assessment
◇ Security Assessment and Authorisation
◇ System and Services Acquisition
◇ System and Communication Protection
◇ System and Information integrity
• Framework for each family of control is composed of following elements
◇ Control
◇ Supplement Guidance
◇ Control enhancements
◇ References
◇ Priority and Baseline allocation
• NIST 800-53A designed to conduct assessments within a risk management framework and provides the following
• Information about effectiveness of security controls applied
• Proof of quality of risk management process in use
• Information about strengths and weakness of information systems
