ISO/IEC 27001


• Worldwide known ISO 27001:2013
• Best known specification in ISMS family of standards
• ISO/IEC 27001 “specifies the requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining and continually improving an information security management system within the context of organisation. It also includes the requirements for assessment and treatment of information security risks tailored to the needs of the organisation”
• Contain accepted good practices and provides accepted baseline against IT audits
• Specifies auditable requirements for establishing, applying, operating, maintaining, reviewing, monitoring and improving the control framework based on organisation information security risk
• Requirements established in ISO/IEC 27001 cover all styles of organisation such as large-scale and small-scale
• Although ISO does not perform certifications, it is common for organisations to asset that product or system is certified to an ISO standard
• Contents of ISO/IEC 27001 made up of following sections
◇ Introduction
◇ Scope
◇ Normative References
◇ Terms and Definitions
