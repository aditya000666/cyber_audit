What is ISACA


• Information System Audit and Control Association
• Guidance on auditing of computer systems
• Provide professional certification for information system auditors and IT security and governance professionals
• Also provide resources to IT Professionals for conducting audits
• Frameworks available from ISACA
◇ COBIT
◇ Information Technology Assurance Framework
◇ Risk IT Framework
◇ Val IT Framework
◇ Business model for information security
