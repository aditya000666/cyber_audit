What is COSO


Committee of Sponsoring Organisation (COSO)



• Founded in 1985
• Part of program to study the reason that lead to fraud in financial reporting
• Today the mission of COSO is to “improve the organisational performance and governing” as well as “reduce the extent of fraud in organisation”
• Many organisations choose COSO as it is recognised by Security and Exchange Commission (SEC) and identified as a suitable framework within additional regulator around SOX
• COSO addresses risk management across organisation
• Steps followed in COSO
◇ Risk Management
◇ Internal Controls
◇ Fraud Deterence
• COSO issued a framework “Enterprise Risk Management”, to examine the risk within the organisation and apply risk-based processes
