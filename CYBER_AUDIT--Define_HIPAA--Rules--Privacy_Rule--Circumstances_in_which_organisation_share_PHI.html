Circumstances in which organisation share PHI


• May share the protected information if it has a court order
• Includes order of administrative tribunal
• Provider may only disclose the information described in order
• Subpoena issued by someone other than judge is different from court order
• Health care provider can only share the PHI in case of subpoena only if notification requirements of Privacy Rule are met
• Notify the person who is subject of information about the request so person has change to object to disclosure
• Health care provider give notice that tells how the PHI will be used and shared
• Include the health privacy rights
• Person can ask for a copy at any time
