How to select a Standard


• Organisations first select frameworks most appropriate to their business
• Auditor job is to evaluate whether the company selected standard is reasonable
• Select a standard that can be followed, this allows the standards to be more easily put in place. It also allows others within the organisation and auditors to embrace the standards.
• Employ the standard, this reduces liability for having a selected specific standard that is not actually put into place
• Select a flexible standard, this provides organisation the ability to remain responsive to changing business environments and consider own risk profile


Standards are relevant to individuals and groups within the organisation
