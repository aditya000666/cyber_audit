Meeting Stakeholder needs


• An enterprise exist to create value for stakeholders
• Value that is created must be done so in a way that also considers risk and the appropriate use of resources
• The needs of stakeholders are influenced by of number of different drivers
• These needs are divided into 3 categories:
◇ Benefits realisation
◇ Risk optimisation
◇ Resource optimisation
• Based on the needs enterprise goals need to be determined across 4 dimensions
◇ Financial
◇ Customer
◇ Internal
◇ Learning and Growth
