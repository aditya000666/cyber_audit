Process to Reduce Risk


• The process of identifying, assessing and reducing risks to an acceptable level involves
• Defines and controls threats and vulnerabilities
• Implement risk reduction measures

• An analytic discipline with 3 parts:
◇ Risk Assessment: Determine what the risks are
◇ Risk Management: Evaluating alternatives for mitigating the risk
◇ Risk Communication: Presenting this material in understandable way to decision makers and/or the public
