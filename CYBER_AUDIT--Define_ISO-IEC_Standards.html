Define ISO/IEC Standards


• International Organisation of Standards is non government group that brings both private and public sectors together and create solutions for business and society
• ISO technical committee establish major support and global relevance
• ISO/IEC 27000 series of standards and related terms provide guidance on matters of information security
• This includes implementing, designing and auditing an Information Security Management System (ISMS). ISMS describe policies, standards and programs related to information security
