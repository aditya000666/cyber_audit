Business need for Continuity Planning


• Provide immediate response to emergency situation
• Protect lives and ensure safety
• Reduce business impact
• Resume critical business functions
• Reduce confusion during crisis
• Ensure survivability of business
• Get up and running services after disaster

• Business Resumption Planning develops procedures to initiate recovery of business operations immediately following and outage or disaster
• Contingency Plan is a document providing procedures for recovering a major application or information system network in event of outage or disaster
• Continuity of Operation Plan is a document describing procedures and capabilities to sustain organisation essential strategic functions at alternate site
• Critical Business Functions must be restored immediately to ensure organisation assets are protected, goals met and organisation is in compliance with any regulation and legal responsibilities
