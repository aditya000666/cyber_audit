Operations Security Policy


• Ensure correct and secure operations of information processing facilities
• Documented procedure should be prepared for operational activities with information processing and communication facilities
• Major points include
◇ Change management
◇ Capacity management
◇ Separation of development, testing and operational environment
◇ Protection from malware
◇ Backup
◇ Logging and Monitoring
◇ Protection of log information
◇ Administrator and Operator logs
◇ Clock synchronisation
◇ Control of operation software
◇ Technical vulnerability management
