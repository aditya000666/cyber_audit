Information Security Incident Management Policy


• Ensure consistent and effective approach to management of information security incident, including communication on security event and weakness
• Procedure for incident response planning and preparation
• Procedure for monitoring, detecting, analysing and reporting of information security events and incident
• Procedure for logging incident management activities
• Procedure for handling forensic evidence
• Reporting information security events
• Reporting information security weakness
• Response to information security incidents
• Learning from information security incident
• Collection of evidence
