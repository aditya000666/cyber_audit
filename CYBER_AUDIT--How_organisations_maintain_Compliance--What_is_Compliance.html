What is Compliance


• All relevant legislative statutory, regulatory requirements and organisation approach to meet these requirement should be explicitly identified, documented and kept up to date for each information system
• Identification of applicable legislation and contractual requirement
• Protection of records
• Intellectual property rights
• Information security review
• Independent review of information security
• Compliance with security policies and standards
• Technical compliance review
• Should be performed with the use of automated tools
• Technical compliance review should be carried out by competent, authorised persons
