Goals


• Integrate ERM within organisation performance management cycle
• Communicate benefits of risk management
• Convey Organisation policy, approach and attitude to risk management
• Set scope and application of risk management within the organisation
• Establish roles and responsibilities for managing risk
• Set out consistent approach for managing risks across organisation
• Detail the process for escalating and reporting risks
• Convey Organisation commitment to periodic review and verification of Framework and continual improvement
• Ensure department meets its risk reporting obligations
