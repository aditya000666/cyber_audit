Describe about Risk Management


• Risk Management involves identification of organisation information assets and development, documentation, implementation, and updating of policies, standards, procedures and guidelines that ensure CIA.
• Management tools such as data classification, risk assessment and analysis are used to identify threats, classify assets and rate the vulnerabilities so that effective security measures and controls are implemented
• Probability a particular threat will exploit a particular vulnerability
• Need to systematically understand the risks to a system and decide how to control them
