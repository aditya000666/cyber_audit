Disaster Recovery Planning


• Contains procedures for emergency response, extended backup operations and post-disaster recovery
• Primary objective of disaster recovery plan is to provide capability to process mission essential application in degraded mode and return to normal mode of operation within reasonable amount of time
• This is a subset of BCP
• The goal of DRP is to minimise the effects of disaster and take necessary steps to ensure that resources, personnel and business processes are able to resume operation in timely manner
• Disaster recovery address issues in terms of recovery plan development, implementation and restoration
