Define about Business Continuity Planning and Disaster Recovery Planning


• Business Continuity Planning and Disaster Recovery Planning address the preservation of business in face of major disruptions to normal business operations
• BCP and DRP involve the preparation, testing and updating of specific actions to protect critical business process from effect of major system and network failures
• BCP helps to identify organisation exposure to internal and external threats
• BCP counteracts interruptions to business activities and should be available to protect critical business processes from effects of major failure or disasters
