Business Impact Analysis


• Purpose of BIA is to provide written documentation to assist organisation management in understanding the business impact associated with possible outages.
• Identify organisation business function and associated information system to determine the criticality
• Prioritise critical information system
• Analyse impact of outage such as loss of revenue, additional operating expenses, delay of income
• It is a management level function identifies the impact to business operations
• BIA leverages information from risk assessment

• Impact is measured by some attributes
◇ Tangible attribute
◇ Intangible attribute
