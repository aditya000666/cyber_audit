Privacy Rule


• Privacy rules set national standards for when Protected Health Information (PHI) be used and disclosed
• Privacy rule applies to PHI in any form
• Includes computer and paper files, X-Rays, Physician appointment schedules, Medical bills, Dictated notes, Conversations and Information entered into patient portal
• Gives patient more control over their health information
• Establish appropriate safeguard that health care providers must achieve to protect privacy of health information
• Holds violator accountable for civil and criminal penalties upon violation of Privacy Rights
